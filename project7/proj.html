Project Gallery


Below are previous projects I have completed for classes. The projects are linked for easier viewing.




LIN406 Field Methods Research Poster

Led by Dr. Adam Singerman, the class used field methods with native speaker consultant William Andahua to discover a general understanding of the broad characteristics of Ancash Quechua.

IST341 Human Centered Design Prototype

Throughout the semester, we designed a website that allows college students to build a meal schedule, implementing user research and utilizing the design process.

LIN431 Phonological Analysis Research Poster

Led by Dr. Chris Green, the class phonologically analyzed raw data on Nupoid languages, as collected by Dr. Weise and her team in Nigeria.

LIN406 Field Methods Research Paper

After intial findings, Loretta Awuku and I paired up to elicit more data on verbal morphology and syncretism in Ancash Quechua.

IST256 Final Project

At the end of the semester, I coded a system that would produce links to different playlists and music videos using sentiment analysis of a person's horoscope for the day.

LIN202 Language Ambassador Project

Using the concepts taught in class, I created a brief overview of the Irish language and its properties.
